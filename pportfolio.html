Circuitry of Fright - Is Public Opinion of ECT Justified?

This essay was built upon the short essay "Insanity of 'The Mind Electric'", and so was also for the same class. For this I expanded upon the previous project by researching electroconvulsive therapy (ECT) to find out the real side-effects of the treatment. During my research I found out that the side-effects are rather minimal, and that the public image of ECT is overly negative. In this essay I argue why ECT should be more widely accepted because it can really help many people with several detrimental mental disorders.
