The Alien of the East Prologue

This was written for the class Intro to Creative Writing, and more importantly, is the prologue of a story I intend to develop further. The assignment for the class was simply to write a creative writing piece to be workshopped in the classroom. I knew I already wanted to write this, so it was easy to decide on what to do. While writing this, I focused on creative vivid imagery, alongside telling the story, which helped me practice writing prose at a higher level. I am very proud of this piece, as I believe it showcases my skills in writing powerful and evocative prose.

The Rays of the Sun

This three-page short story was written in an online English club. It was written off of the prompt: “Your character heads to the beach and expects to have a fun day splashing in the waves. When they get there though, they find that the water is gone, or at least it’s not in sight.” I think this story displays skill in vague first-person storytelling, forming a narrative that is open to interpretation.
